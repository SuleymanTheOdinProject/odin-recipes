Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar Salad is a classic dish featuring crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a tangy Caesar dressing. It's a refreshing and flavorful salad that makes a great side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add the lettuce to the bowl and toss with Caesar dressing until well coated.
  3. Add croutons and toss gently.
  4. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
