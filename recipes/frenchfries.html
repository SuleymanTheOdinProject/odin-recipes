Classic French Fries

Description

Classic French fries are crispy, golden, and delicious. They're a perfect side dish and can be seasoned to taste. They’re easy to make at home and are a favorite comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt
  • Pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Rinse and soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Drain and pat potatoes dry with paper towels.
  5. Fry potato strips in batches until golden and crispy, about 4-5 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove fries and drain on paper towels. Season with salt and pepper if desired.
