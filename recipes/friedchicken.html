Skillet fried chicken breast

Description

Skillet fried chicken breast is a delicious and simple dish where chicken breasts are pan-fried to perfection. It tastes good, contains 50+ grams of protein, is cheap and easy

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour oil into skillet and wait for 2 minutes
  2. Put one chicken breast into skillet
  3. Wait 5 minutes
  4. Flip
  5. Add some salt and paprika
